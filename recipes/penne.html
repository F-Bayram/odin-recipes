Description

Penne is an extruded type of pasta with cylinder-shaped pieces, their ends cut at an angle

Arrabbiata sauce is a spicy sauce for pasta made from garlic, tomatoes, and dried red chili peppers cooked in olive oil.

Ingredients

  • Penne
  • Tomato paste
  • Onions
  • Chili peppers
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Olive oil
  • Pecorino cheese
  • Parsley
  1. Cook the penne in hot salted water
  2. Chop the garlic
  3. Heat the olive oil and sweat the chopped garlic and the chili flock
  4. Simmer the tomato paste
  5. Put the pasta water and the cooked penne into the tomato sauce, so they mix up
  6. Sprinkle with pecorino cheese and parsley, drizzle with a bit of olive oil
  7. Enjoy it, but be cautious, it can be a little bit hot for you :)
