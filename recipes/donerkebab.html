Doener Kebab

Description

Doener kebab is a type of kebab, made of meat cooked on a vertical rotisserie.

The vertical rotisserie was invented in the 19th-century Ottoman Empire, and dishes such as the Arab shawarma, Greek gyros, Canadian donair, and Mexican al pastor devired from this.

Ingredients

Various types of ingredients exists for Doener Kebab, so the following list is just like how I would make mine spontaneously.
  • Sliced Meat
  • Bread
  • Onions
  • Red cabbage
  • Cucumbers
  • Tomatoes
  • Salad
  • Turkish feta cheese
  • Pepper
  • Turkish tzatziki

Steps

  1. Bake the bread
  2. Cook the meat
  3. Put the meat on the bread
  4. Choose vegetables and put them sequentially on the bread
  5. Choose one or more sauces -mostly tzatziki is included-, and put it on the bread
  6. Optionally, add spicing like "pul biber"
  7. Eat it, but simultaneously with ayran for a great experience!
